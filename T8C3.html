The Da Vinci Mystery

Decipher the secret behind a legendary codebreaker.

Clue 4: "My work was shrouded in mystery, not to be understood until decades after my death. I left a cryptic message behind, but I am not a criminal. My creations transcended time and place, and some say I even foresaw the future. Who am I?"

This individual was a brilliant mind, whose work remained obscure and misunderstood during their lifetime. Though they created groundbreaking inventions and theories, their contributions were not fully appreciated until much later. Their legacy has inspired countless fields of study, but their life was marred by secrecy and mystery. Who is this visionary who saw beyond their time?

Correct! The answer is "Nikola Tesla," the visionary inventor and electrical engineer. Though Tesla's innovations paved the way for modern technology, his genius was largely unrecognized during his lifetime. Many of his discoveries, such as wireless communication and alternating current, were ahead of their time.
